# Regulatory And Regional Context

## Why This Matters

Secure Access Stack is shaped by operating realities in Guatemala and LATAM, where security architecture must account for:

- cost sensitivity
- supportability by small technical teams
- hybrid or self-hosted infrastructure constraints
- audit and control expectations

## Architecture Themes Influenced By This Context

- stronger perimeter control for exposed systems
- identity hardening for remote and administrative access
- auditable operational records and evidence trails
- encryption-aware handling of sensitive systems and data
- deployment models that reduce dependency on external platforms

## Public Positioning

The repository does not claim formal regulatory certification. Instead, it documents how regional compliance pressure and operational reality can influence architecture choices and product framing.
