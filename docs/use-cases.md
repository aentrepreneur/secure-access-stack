# Secure Access Stack Use Cases

## Primary Use Cases

- MFA for VPN access
- hardening of administrative portals
- directory-backed authentication flows
- compliance-oriented identity control

## Customer Scenarios

### Small Business With Remote Access

- strengthen VPN access for distributed staff
- reduce dependency on password-only authentication
- keep operational ownership inside the business or local provider

### SMB With Internal Administrative Systems

- harden dashboards and back-office systems
- centralize stronger authentication logic
- improve traceability for privileged access

### Compliance-Sensitive Environment

- introduce stronger identity assurance
- make access controls easier to explain to auditors and stakeholders
- improve evidence posture for access-related reviews

## Business Outcomes

- lower exposure to credential misuse
- lower operational friction
- better audit posture
- stronger identity control with local operational ownership

## Why These Use Cases Matter

The differentiator is not only the MFA feature set. The differentiator is the combination of deployment speed, maintainability, local fit, and productized delivery.
